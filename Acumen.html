ACUMEN

The most participated category of Tech-Tatva.Acumen is the most fun and thrilling category in the Tech Tatva. It has 4 events with an amazing participation throughout. The events are ATR, AIB, MIQ and Hopeless Opus.
GLIMPSES
STATS
Earn Pocket Money

One can win upto Rs 10,000 by participating in the ACUMEN. By just tresure hunt, some simple Science quizes and experiment, Acumen provide you a easy win over other Category.

Connect To People

Acumen is a highest participating category in TechTatva. Every year more than 1000 students take part in Acumen. No other Category is able to surpass this record.

Reach Your Top

ACUMEN provides a platform for aptitude and intellect. The events require participants to showcase the skills of critical analysis and out-of-box thinking to slice through complex problems, treasure hunts, and puzzles.

EVENTS
A treasure hunt like no other

AMAZING TECH RACE

Run around manipal in this amazing treasure hunt.There are 2 rounds where you race against time using both mind and muscles and find treasure around you in the campus.

TEST YOUR IQ

MANIPAL INTELLIGENCE QUOTIENT

Exercise your IQ in the maze of puzzles.MIQ has 2 rounds . Here you wake up Garry Kasparov in yourself to make calculated moves through a series of puzzles and games and show off your presence of IQ.

NOT THE AIB YOU KNOW

AIB

This event tests participants based on their ability to deduce solutions to problems from basic knowledge of science and technology.AIB has 3 rounds where you show your love for technology in Manipal's most exciting Tech quiz. The teams contain 2 to 3 members and you apply science of deduction and navigation through science of trivia.

THE ONLINE ONE

HOPELESS OPUS

HOPELESS OPUS is an online event in Acumen. It has some amazing online race where the gamers face 3 options where they are introduced to osteoporosis apocalyptic world. These options are of three kinds having direct solutions, long paths and dead ends. This event contains no pen and paper and is purely online. An online story based event which will test your knowledge of your campus as wellas make you scratch your heads
