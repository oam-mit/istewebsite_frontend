A Brief Summary Of Our Events

In the last one year, we had events ranging from various internal technical workshops to many external workshops. Previous year began with a website development workshop that attracted a huge crowd, which was followed by the ManComm recruitments. ACUMEN : The category organised by ISTE, is the category with highest participation in TechTatva. It had several events within it which included MIQ, AIB, Hopeless Opus and ATR.
WORKSHOPS: Next we conducted a series of successful workshop that got in a huge number of students from different years and many branches. One such workshop was in collaboration with Amazon on how to code Alexa to do different tasks that she does!
WINTER SCHOOL: Winter Vacations in Manipal is incomplete without ISTE’s Winter School. It is where we get online and teach interested people different skills ranging from coding to app dev to graphics. E-certificates are provided to those who perform well!
TECH TATVA: Wattup, Codestorm, Clueless, Unlockathon and Exquisite – these are a few events that will get you to scratch your brain to the last brain cell, both individually as well as in a team. And that’s what we do best.
SUMMER SCHOOL: Enters summer vacation. We made good use of that too. We conducted Summer School just like Winter School and help students learn Web Development, App Development, Graphic Designing, Coding essentials like Python, Data Structures etc. THE TECHNICAL PROPHET: One of the most imperative things that we had done this year was to start out own blog-The Technical Prophet In between all this, we even organised several internal workshops for WC and ManComm to learn from vaious achievers in their respective fields. .
