Our Events

A brief Summary of what our events are about

In the last one year, we had events ranging from various internal technical workshops to many external workshops. The previous year began with a website development workshop that attracted a huge crowd, which was followed by the ManComm recruitments. Acumen, the category organised by ISTE, is the category with highest participation in TechTatva. It had several events within it which included MIQ, AIB, Hopeless Opus and ATR Next we had the most successful workshop that got in a huge number of students from different years and many branches. We also organised the winter school which was an online school where we taught different skills and also provided e-certificates to those who performed well. We then organised a tech week which had different categories including Wattup, Codestorm, Clueless, Unlockathon and Exquisite. We then had an Alexa workshop that again attracted a huge number of participants. This was followed by summer school which had many domains for people of different interests. It had WebDev, Python, Graphics, AppDev , Data Structures. One of the most imperative things that we had done this year was to start out own blog- THE TECHNICAL PROPHET. In between all this, we even organised several internal workshops for WC and ManComm to learn from vaious achievers in their respective fields.
